# 🏥 Enterprise Healthcare Analytics & Operational Insights Dashboard

> **Transforming raw healthcare data into intelligent, data-driven decisions.**
>
> A comprehensive healthcare analytics solution designed using **Power BI**, **Power Query**, **Excel**, and **DAX** to deliver actionable insights into patient care, hospital performance, billing trends, insurance coverage, and clinical outcomes through an interactive executive dashboard.

---

# 🌟 Project Overview

Healthcare organizations generate massive volumes of operational and clinical data every day. Without proper analysis, valuable insights remain hidden.

This project demonstrates a complete **end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow**, transforming raw healthcare records into an interactive dashboard that empowers stakeholders to monitor hospital performance, patient distribution, insurance patterns, medical conditions, and financial metrics for informed decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Improve visibility into hospital operations

✔ Analyze patient demographics & medical conditions

✔ Evaluate insurance coverage and billing trends

✔ Monitor doctor workload & hospital performance

✔ Support strategic healthcare decision-making

✔ Deliver an interactive executive dashboard

# 📈 Business Insights

💡 Emergency and Elective admissions contribute the largest share of patient volume.

💡 Billing is influenced more by treatment complexity than patient age.

💡 Longer hospital stays generate significantly higher billing amounts.

💡 A small number of insurance providers account for the majority of healthcare revenue.

💡 Doctor workload is unevenly distributed across hospitals.

💡 Patient test results reveal meaningful patterns that support healthcare quality assessment.

💡 Interactive analytics enable faster operational and strategic decision-making.
